My Favorite Albums

Here is a list of the albums I have had on repeat this year

Ferite by Capo Plaza

Ferite is an album by Capo Plaza. During these months, I have had it on repeat. The album is diverse, featuring everything from drill, rap, and hip hop to even pop. On this album, there is a song for everyone.

Chase Atlantic by Chase Atlantic

Chase Atlantic, just like Ferite, is very diverse. It is an album you will never get sick of. I have been listening to this album since 2020, and I fall in love with it every single time I listen to it. It is simply timeless.

Épico by Lunay

Épico is Lunay's first full-length album. It has one of my favorite songs by him, "360". By now, you might have guessed, but this album is also very diverse. It is a blend of reggaeton, Latin trap, and Latin pop. It is simply a very upbeat and fun album. which is perfect for the summer.
